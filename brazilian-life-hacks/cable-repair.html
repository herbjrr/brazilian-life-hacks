Cable repair

Still works. Just need to find the correct position.

Nobody likes when cables break.

This ancient Brazilian technique will help you overcome this annoying problem.

What do you need?

  • A broken cable
  • Packing tape

Steps

  1. Find the correct position that makes the cable work;
  2. Put enough tape to fix the position;
  3. Avoid moving the cable or you will have to redo the repair.

The nail and the flip flops

The most famous Brazilian life hack

This is an ancient technique. It is said that Brazilian born knowing how to fix a broken flip flops.

Seriously, every Brazilian have made this once in life.

What do you need?

  • Broken flip flops
  • A nail

Steps

  1. Place the nail carefully in the broken part of the flip flops;
  2. Ensure that the nail is firmly stuck in the flip flops;
  3. You better have emergency nails, in case the flip flops break again.

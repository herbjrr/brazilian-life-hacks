Powerful cooler

Who needs water coolers?

Brazil's tropical climate affects negatively the PC components.

Fortunately, fans are common in Brazilian homes.

What do you need?

  • Overheated computer
  • A fan (the bigger, the better)

Steps

  1. Open the computer cabinet;
  2. place the fan facing the motherboard;
  3. Enjoy the performance boost.
